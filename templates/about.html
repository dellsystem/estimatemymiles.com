About

What is this site?

estimatemymiles.com is a tool that allows frequent flyers to make informed decisions about which flight to take in order to maximise the mileage accrued. Whether you're saving miles for a round-the-world trip, or trying to meet an elite qualification threshold, or simply want to optimise your flight plans to earn more miles, estimatemymiles.com can help you get there.

Why is estimatemymiles.com needed?

If you only ever fly and earn with a single airline, then your choices are pretty clear-cut, and this site is unlikely to help you. However, if you earn miles with an airline that has many earning partners, then the amount of choice you have increases exponentially. For example, if you earn with a Star Alliance airline, such as Air Canada, then you can accrue miles with that airline when you fly any Star Alliance airline.

Sounds great, right? Well, it's not that simple. Each airline has their own set of rules for how many miles you'll accrue when flying on other airlines within the alliance, and these rules can depend on anything from the fare class, to the origin and destination, to the phase of the moon during your flight. (Actually, no airline does that last one. Yet.) These rules are usually displayed on the earning airline's website in the form of a chart, often with a multitude of footnotes that require quite a bit of intense scrutiny to be fully understood. What's more, if you earn with multiple airlines within an alliance, you'll have to investigate each airline's website separately if you want to know how many miles you'll earn for a particular flight with each airline.

That's where estimatemymiles.com comes in. Not only do we store mileage accrual rules for multiple airlines (and their partners!) - eliminating the need to have 5 different tabs open as you compare charts - we simplify the lookup process to make it as easy as possible for users to understand each airline's accrual rules.
