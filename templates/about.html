About

EstimateMyMiles is a free service designed to make it as easy as possible to figure out how many miles you'll earn on your flights with any frequent flyer program. Instead of having to trawl through countless airline websites to figure out which program you should credit your miles to, or which airline you should fly to get the most miles for a given flight, simply use this website. With EstimateMyMiles, you can optimise your travel plans for achieving elite status or accruing the miles you need to book your next vacation.
